I am working on a separate Replit project called TinyCommand AI. Our tech stack:

Frontend: React 19, Vite, TailwindCSS v4, shadcn/ui, GoJS for canvas views
Backend: Express.js, Node.js

AI: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini with tool-calling (function calling)
